About Environmental Science (ES)

Environmental Science (ES) is a theoretical and awareness-driven subject that introduces the fundamentals of the environment, ecological balance, and sustainable development.

It aims to create awareness about environmental issues, laws, and conservation techniques. It is useful in understanding climate change, pollution control, biodiversity, and sustainability.
