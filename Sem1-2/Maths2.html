About Maths-2 (Mathematics-2)

Mathematics-2 is a theoretical and analytical subject in the first year of Computer Engineering. It focuses on advanced mathematical concepts like calculus, differential equations, and complex numbers that build the foundation for future technical subjects.

The study of Mathematics-2 enhances problem-solving skills and logical thinking essential for engineering fields like data science, algorithms, machine learning, and simulations.

This page provides syllabus, past papers, solutions, notes, and extra materials to help you master Maths-2 easily.
