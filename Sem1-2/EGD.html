About Engineering Graphics & Design (EGD)

Engineering Graphics & Design (EGD) is a visual and practical subject that teaches students how to represent objects, designs, and structures using technical drawings.

It is essential for understanding the language of engineering drawings, orthographic projections, isometric views, and geometric constructions.

The study of EGD builds skills required for careers in design, architecture, manufacturing, and mechanical fields. Here you'll find all necessary materials, papers, sheets, and notes to make this subject easier.
