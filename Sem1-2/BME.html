About Basic Mechanical Engineering (BME)

Basic Mechanical Engineering (BME) is a fundamental theoretical subject that introduces the basic principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and energy systems.

It is useful in fields related to machines, engines, manufacturing, and thermal systems. This subject builds foundational knowledge that is helpful for both mechanical and interdisciplinary engineering applications.

All essential materials, papers, and notes for BME are provided here to help you succeed in your exams.
