About Basic Electrical Engineering (BEE)

Basic Electrical Engineering (BEE) is a fundamental theoretical subject that introduces the basics of electrical circuits, machines, and power systems.

This subject is essential for understanding current, voltage, resistance, AC/DC circuits, transformers, and motors. It lays the groundwork for future studies in electronics, electrical machines, and energy systems.

Here you’ll find all the necessary materials, notes, and papers to help you learn and score well in BEE.
