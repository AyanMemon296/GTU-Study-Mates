About Maths-1 (Mathematics-1)

Maths-1 (Mathematics-1) is a mathematical and theoretical subject that forms the foundation for all technical and engineering studies. It includes concepts like calculus, matrices, and differential equations which are used in almost every engineering field.

This subject improves problem-solving skills, analytical thinking, and logical reasoning — essential for any technical career.
