About Workshop

Workshop is a practical and skill-based subject in the first year of engineering. It focuses on hands-on learning, basic manufacturing processes, and safety measures in engineering workshops.

It helps students develop an understanding of different tools, machines, and fabrication processes that form the foundation for mechanical and manufacturing industries.
