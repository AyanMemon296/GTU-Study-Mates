About TOC (Theory of Computation)

TOC (Theory of Computation) is a theoretical subject that focuses on understanding the fundamentals of automata, formal languages, and computational problems.

This subject is essential for compiler design, algorithms, artificial intelligence, and understanding the limits of what can be computed.
