About MPI (Microprocessor & Interfacing)

MPI (Microprocessor & Interfacing) is a theoretical and practical subject that focuses on how microprocessors work internally, how they process instructions, and how they can be connected with external devices.

This subject is essential for embedded systems, hardware programming, and system design.
