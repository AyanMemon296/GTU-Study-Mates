About GTU Study Mates

Your Ultimate Free Learning Platform for GTU Computer Engineering

What is GTU Study Mates?

GTU Study Mates is a free educational platform created by Ayan Memon, designed specifically for students of Gujarat Technological University (GTU) pursuing Computer Engineering, CS, CSE, and IT branches. It provides syllabus-based study materials, solved papers, handwritten notes, and much more - all in one place.


Our Vision

We aim to build a community-driven learning platform where students can access comprehensive materials, share knowledge, and grow together - making education accessible to all without barriers.


Why GTU Study Mates?

Many students struggle to find complete and reliable resources for GTU subjects. Materials are often scattered, outdated, or behind paywalls. GTU Study Mates solves this by offering everything you need in one place in an organized, syllabus-focused, and completely free manner.

The platform is built by students, for students, to help with semester exams, vivas, assignments, and projects.


What We Offer

  • YouTube Playlists covering syllabus-based lectures and paper solutions
  • GTU Papers and Paper Solutions
  • IMP Questions, IMP QNA, MIMP QNA
  • Syllabus PDFs, Lab Manuals, Handwritten Notes
  • GTU Books, PPTs, and Extra Materials
  • Project Guidance and more
