Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Privacy is Our Priority

1. Introduction

Welcome to GTU Study Mates. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect minimal information such as your name, email address, or feedback when you contact us through the Contact Form. We do not collect any sensitive or unnecessary personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used only to:

  • Respond to your queries or feedback
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you important updates if requested

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is strictly used for communication between you and GTU Study Mates.

5. External Links

Our website contains links to external sites like YouTube, Google Drive, etc. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate steps to protect your personal information, but please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend visiting this page periodically to stay informed about any changes.

8. Contact

If you have any concerns about privacy or data usage, please feel free to Contact Us through our dedicated page.
