About CS (Cyber Security)

Cyber Security (CS) focuses on protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats, attacks, and unauthorized access.

This subject is essential in today's digital world to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

You will learn various security principles, tools, techniques, and real-life applications.
