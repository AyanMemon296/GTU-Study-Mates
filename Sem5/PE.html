About PE (Professional Ethics)

Professional Ethics (PE) helps students understand ethical principles, professional responsibilities, and moral values essential in the field of engineering and technology.

This subject strengthens your knowledge about code of conduct, decision-making, and social responsibilities as a future engineer.
