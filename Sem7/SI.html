About SI (Summer Internship)

The duration of internship will be two weeks. It will be after completion of 6th Semester and before the commencement of Semester VII.

The details of the type of internship carried out by the student will be uploaded on the GTU PMMS portal.

Available internship types include: Offline Internship, Online Internship, or Mini Project.

A Summer Internship report must be uploaded on the PMMS portal after completion.
