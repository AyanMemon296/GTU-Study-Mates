About MCWC (Mobile Computing & Wireless Communication)

Mobile Computing & Wireless Communication (MCWC) deals with technologies and protocols that allow computing devices to be used wirelessly across different networks.

It is essential for understanding mobile app backend, wireless protocols, network mobility, and pervasive computing.
