Semester 7 - Computer Engineering

Semester 7 explores advanced and trending fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, and Mobile Development. This semester also includes subjects that strengthen your theoretical base and practical skills.

Find all your Semester 7 study materials, playlists, and reference content below.

Note: In this semester, you are required to complete a 15-day internship. It must be done after the 6th Semester and before the start of the 7th Semester. You will also have to register the project or internship activity on the GTU PMMS Portal. From the given options like Offline Internship, Online Internship, or Mini Project, choose the one that applies. Additionally, you must upload the Summer Internship Report on the GTU PMMS Portal for final submission and evaluation.
