About DBMS (Database Management Systems)

DBMS is a theoretical and practical subject that teaches how to store, manage, and retrieve data in computer systems. It is one of the core subjects in Computer Engineering.

This subject is useful in software development, data management, backend development, and big data fields.

Here you’ll find all the essential study materials, papers, and resources to help you succeed in DBMS.
