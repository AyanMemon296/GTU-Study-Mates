About ETC (Effective Technical Communication)

Effective Technical Communication (ETC) is a theoretical and practical subject that helps students improve their professional communication, writing, and presentation skills.

This subject is useful in fields like software development, project management, business communication, and technical documentation.

Here you’ll find all the essential study materials, papers, and resources to help you succeed in ETC.
