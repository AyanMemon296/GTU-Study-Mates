About COA (Computer Organization & Architecture)

COA (Computer Organization & Architecture) is a theoretical and practical subject that teaches how computers work internally, including how instructions are processed, memory is organized, and hardware is controlled.

This subject is useful in computer hardware design, system architecture, embedded systems, and low-level programming.
