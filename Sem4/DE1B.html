About DE 1B (Design Engineering 1B)

Design Engineering 1B (DE 1B) is a practical and innovative subject that focuses on problem-solving, project development, and design thinking in Computer Engineering.

This subject helps you develop real-world problem-solving skills, project execution, and creativity, which are essential for your academic and professional growth.
